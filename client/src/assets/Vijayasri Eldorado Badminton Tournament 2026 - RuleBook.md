### **III. ‚ö° The "Intact Duration" Deuce Rule**

To ensure the tournament stays on schedule and not one match drags on indefinitely, the following Deuce rule is implemented:

> **If a game reaches a deuce (e.g., 10-10 or 14-14):**
> 1. Only **one** Deuce + Advantage cycle is allowed.
> 2. If the score becomes tied again (entering a **Second Deuce**), the match moves to a **Golden Point**.
> 3. Whoever wins the immediate next point (is considered the **Golden Point**) wins the game, irrespective of who won the first advantage round.

---

### **IV. The 3-Level Tie-Breaker Rule**

If two or more players/teams are tied on "Match Wins" at the end of the League Stage, the following granular rules apply in order:

* **Level 1 (Head-to-Head):** The winner of the match between the two tied players/teams ranks higher.
* **Level 2 (Net Point Difference):** If 3 players are tied, we calculate: **(Total Points Won in all group matches) minus (Total Points Lost in all group matches)**. The highest positive number wins.
* **Level 3 (Total Points Scored):** If still tied, the player who scored the highest total number of points throughout the league stage ranks higher.

---

### **V. Qualification & Progression**

* **Kids Singles (Boys):** 4 Groups. Only the **Winner (Rank 1)** of each group advances to Semi-Finals.
* **Kids Singles (Girls) & Adult Singles:** 2 Groups. **Top 2** from each group advance to Semi-Finals.
* **All Doubles Categories:** Round Robin Pool. **Top 2 teams** in the final points table qualify directly for the Grand Final.

* **Individual Responsibility:** Any additional specific refreshments, sports drinks, or meals must be brought by the individuals.

---

### **VII. Player Conduct & Punctuality**

1. **Reporting:** Players must report to their assigned court **5 minutes prior** to the scheduled slot.
2. **Walkover:** A 5-minute grace period is allowed. Beyond that, the absent player forfeits the match.
3. **Umpiring:** Umpire decisions on "In/Out" calls are final. No arguments will be entertained to maintain the spirit of the game.
